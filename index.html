    About Chef's Pan

    Four childhood friends had a dream of starting their own business which will spread happiness. They knew that serving delicious food is a promising way to success. All four joined hotel management and catering school and earned excellent credentials; Roamed around the world learning different food styles and returned home with enriched skills and immense enthusiasm. They decided to start a mini Italian cuisine eatery in an innovative style and the idea of serving on mobile food truck was conceived. Their experience of working closely with Italian and French Chefs in their European stay helped them greatly in designing authentic recipes and keeping standards high. Chef's Pan's first Food truck started near Pimple Saudagar, Pune in 2015 and within a few months they added another location to accommodate increased demand. Proud to say that anyone who eats at Chef's Pan appreciates the food and returns back with new friends.

    Chef's Pan is also in catering business. They have served to Birthday parties, Sangeet events, Corporate events, Social parties, Exhibitions, etc. and have received appreciations. Business is growing very fast with returning customers and mouth publicity. Explore the catering section to find more details.
